Common Questions

Everything you need to know to get started.

Are all meals strictly vegan?
Yes, 100%. We do not use any animal products, including honey or dairy. Our facilities are exclusively plant-based to prevent cross-contamination.
Can I skip a week or cancel anytime?
Absolutely. Flexibility is core to our service. You can skip, pause, or cancel your subscription through your dashboard before the weekly cutoff.
How are the meals delivered?
We use insulated, eco-friendly boxes with plant-based cooling packs. Your food stays at the safe temperature until you get home.
