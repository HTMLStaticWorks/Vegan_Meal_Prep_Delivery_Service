Billing & Plans FAQ

When will I be charged?
Subscriptions are billed weekly or monthly depending on your plan. Billing occurs exactly 48 hours before your scheduled delivery day.
Can I switch plans anytime?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan through your dashboard. Changes take effect on the next billing cycle.
Are there any hidden delivery fees?
No. All our plans include standard shipping cost. Premium Pro members also get express shipping at no extra cost.
