Support Center FAQ

Quick answers to our most common support requests.

What is the cutoff for weekly modifications?
All changes to your weekly menu or pause requests must be submitted by Thursday 11:59 PM for the following week's delivery.
Do you offer corporate or bulk discounts?
Yes, we have specialized plans for offices and community centers. Please select 'Partnership' in the contact form for a custom quote.
How do I report a missing item?
Please use the 'Subscription Issues' category in the form above. Include your Order ID and our team will issue a refund or replacement within 4 hours.
